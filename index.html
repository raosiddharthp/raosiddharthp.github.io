Saluton Mondo!

This site documents my journey building Generative AI apps for the enterprise.

For each app, I have aimed to document the entire lifecycle of development, starting from Customer interviews and ending with handover to support.

As a TOGAF Certified Enterprise Architect, I have added as much nuance and detail as possible to the various phases of architecting solutions for enterprise grade solutions as the ones demonstrated in this portfolio.

While the Apps themselves are a collections of several tools and technologies, as a Google Certified Cloud Architect, Machine Learning Engineer, and a Gen AI Leader, I have attempted to use every Google Cloud offering through the suite of applications.

My skills however are not just limited to Google Suite of products and you will find each project interspersed with several open source agents, APIs, and services.

Besides the design and development of Apps, I'm also a SAFe certified SPC and this helps me get insights into governance of such projects in the real world. In each project, I have also added details of ARTs and other Agile methodologies that help with release and delivery.

Each Project has its own page with sections relevant to the full lifecycle journey.
