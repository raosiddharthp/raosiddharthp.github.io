Business Strategy: FinRisk Sentinel ‚Äì AI-Driven Financial Crime Intelligence

Enterprise financial crime compliance is currently a "tax on growth." Legacy systems rely on rigid, rule-based logic that triggers 95% false positive rates, burying investigators in manual labor. FinRisk Sentinel shifts the paradigm from reactive "box-ticking" to proactive Risk Intelligence.

1. The Strategic Value Proposition

The Problem: Traditional Fraud/AML systems are "frozen" in static rules, leading to high operational overhead and regulatory friction.

The Solution: Agentic Risk Orchestration. By leveraging a swarm of specialized LLM agents, Sentinel automates the "cognitive heavy lifting" of investigation, narrative writing, and anomaly classification.

The Business Outcome: Reducing "Cost per Alert" while increasing "Regulatory Confidence."

The platform is governed by a Efficiency & Accuracy Index:

$$ROI_{FinRisk} = \frac{(Manual\_Hours\_Saved \times Labor\_Rate) + (Penalty\_Avoidance)}{(Cloud\_OpEx) + (Model\_Inference\_Costs)}$$

False Positive Reduction: By applying BigQuery ML for anomaly detection before the agentic swarm, we filter out noise, ensuring expensive human (and AI) resources focus only on high-probability threats.

4. Implementation Roadmap: The Path to Autonomous Compliance

Following the Crawl-Walk-Run methodology to ensure banking-grade stability:

Phase 1: Real-Time Ingestion (Crawl) ‚Äî Establishing the "Data Nervous System" using Pub/Sub and Dataflow to ingest streaming transaction data into BigQuery.

Phase 2: Augmented Intelligence (Walk) ‚Äî Deploying CrewAI agents to assist human investigators by pre-filling case files and providing "Reasoning Chains" for flagged transactions.

Phase 3: Autonomous Swarm (Run) ‚Äî Full orchestration where agents autonomously query external watchlists, perform link analysis, and draft regulatory filings for final human sign-off.

5. Strategic Architecture: TOGAF ADM Integration

FinRisk Sentinel was architected using the TOGAF ADM to ensure it fits within a complex Tier-1 banking ecosystem.

Phase B (Business): Mapped "Detection-to-Filing" value streams. Identified "Investigative Bottlenecks" as the primary area for GenAI disruption.
