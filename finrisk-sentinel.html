Sentinel utilizes a Hierarchical Supervisor Pattern. A central Supervisor manages specialized workers, ensuring data validation and investigative reasoning happen concurrently without losing context.

If the swarm's confidence score falls below 85%, the system triggers an Escalation Gateway, routing the case brief to a human investigator. This ensures the AI remains a Co-Pilot, never a "Black Box" in safety-critical banking.
